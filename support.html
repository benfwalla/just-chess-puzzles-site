Restoring a purchase

The unlock is a one-time purchase tied to your Apple ID. On a new device, or after reinstalling, open Settings inside the app and tap Restore purchases. You will not be charged again.

The app works offline

All puzzles are stored on your device, so no internet connection is needed to play. If puzzles are not loading, fully close the app and reopen it.

No account needed

There is no sign-up or login. Puzzle history, rating, and settings live only on your device. Deleting the app removes them.

You get 20 free puzzles a day. The one-time unlock removes the daily limit and opens the full set of 100,000+ puzzles for good, with no subscription.
