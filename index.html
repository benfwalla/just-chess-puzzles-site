Everything it does

Puzzles

  • 117,121 rated puzzles from the Lichess Open Puzzle Database, bundled in the app.
  • Works fully offline. No internet is ever needed.
  • 20 free puzzles a day, forever. A one-time $4.99 unlock removes the daily cap.
  • Difficulty adapts to your rating.
  • Underpromotion puzzles included. No auto-queen.

Practice and review

  • Play it out. After you solve, keep playing the position against the built-in engine.
  • Evaluation bar showing how winning or losing the position is.
  • Hints when you want them.
  • An Elo-style rating that updates after every puzzle, with a rating graph over time.
  • Stats by category, so you can see your weakest tactics.

Categories
